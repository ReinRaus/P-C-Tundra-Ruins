Controls

Single click/tap anywhere on screen or single key press: Highlights a random unhighlighted hexagon

Double click/tap or double key press (within 300ms): Resets all hexagons

When all hexagons are highlighted, any single action will reset all hexagons

Random Selection Information

The suggested cells for selection are completely random choices. Your brain chooses non-random sequences and because of this you have to make more moves. A completely random move reduces the total number of moves when there are many such moves.
